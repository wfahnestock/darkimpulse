Welcome to the Dark Impulse site!

We host popular Team Fortress 2 servers that specialize in the best Orange maps and Arena maps to provide a fun, yet challenging environment for both our senior players and the newbies that frequent our server. There are many players that play on the Dark Impulse servers exclusively, members and non-members alike.

To your left is the sidebar with all of our pages, including a Gallery of member works, an Archive section for downloading the various maps or other files that we use on our servers, and a Store, where, if you wish, you may donate money to the clan and become an Admin for a month.

To your right, you will see our Event Board, where we post important meetings, server parties, and any other salient information.

The head cheese, Sir Cheese, does not tolerate hacking, excessive hate-speech or pornography, or flaming on the server, the forums, Ventrilo channels, or in Steam chat. Doing so will result in banning.
